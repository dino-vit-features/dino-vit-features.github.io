Co-segmentation on PASCAL- Co

We present results on 20 randomly chosen sets from our  PASCAL CO dataset (see Fig. 8). We compare unsupervised method (Faktor etal. [1]), supervised methods (DOCS [2], SSNM [3]), saliency baseline, and our results.
